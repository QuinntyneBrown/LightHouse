# L1 — High-Level Requirements: LightHouse Kids UI Design

## L1-1: Child-Facing Home Screen
The platform shall provide a colorful, age-filtered home screen for children that surfaces curated content (videos, audio, playlists) based on the active child profile's age band (Seedlings 0–2, Sprouts 3–5, Explorers 6–9). Navigation must be icon-driven with large touch targets suitable for pre-readers.

## L1-2: Content Playback Experience
The platform shall provide a distraction-free video and audio playback screen with simple child-friendly controls, playlist progression, and no ads or algorithmic recommendations.

## L1-3: Onboarding & Profile Management
The platform shall provide a parent-facing onboarding flow including account creation, child profile setup (name, age, avatar), and a PIN-protected parent settings area.

## L1-4: Parental Controls Dashboard
The platform shall provide a parent dashboard behind PIN protection for managing screen time limits, content preview, viewing history, and content blocking per child profile.

## L1-5: Content Browsing & Playlists
The platform shall provide browsing screens for content categories (Bible Stories, Worship Songs, Prayers, Devotionals, Activities), curated playlists, and search functionality.

## L1-6: Navigation & App Shell
The platform shall provide a consistent mobile-first app shell with tab-based navigation, profile switching, and age-appropriate theming that feels safe, warm, and joyful.
